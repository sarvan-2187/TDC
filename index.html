Internship Description: Website Redesign for Tanjore Degree Coffee


I had the privilege of redesigning the digital home of Tanjore Degree Coffee, a cherished brand known for its authentic South Indian filter coffee and refreshing rose milk. Our mission was to blend tradition with technology - creating a minimalist yet modern experience that honors the brand’s rich legacy.

This redesign features:


1. Smooth parallax scrolling for a dynamic browsing experience

2. Interactive hover effects and animated buttons for user engagement

3. Fully responsive layouts tailored for mobile, tablet, and desktop users

4. Visual storytelling through curated images and video assets

5. Performance optimizations to ensure lightning-fast loading speeds

6. Basic SEO integration for better discoverability

7. My goal is To build a digital experience as rich and memorable as a cup of Tanjore Degree Coffee itself. ☕
